The Team

Rome wasn't built in a day, and neither was our team. With a great mix of enthusiastic go-getters and calm-and-sensible hard workers, the DKGA Group is filled with the right people in the right positions.

The team is headed by feisty and vivacious MD & CEO, Ms Disha Chaudhary who founded the company based on her immense experience in Real Estate and Infrastructure Projects. She has headed companies which has worked on implementing innovative ideas to reutilize waste household water, without compromising on structural safety. The residential projects she worked on came with a host of amenities like indoor swimming with child safety lock, kitchens with adjustable height platforms, gymnasiums, baby crèches and a whole lot more.

Her vision is to help people manage their portfolios over a diverse range of offerings by leading a group of highly motivated people.
